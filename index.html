Who We Are
Ridgeant Technologies Canada Inc. is a trusted IT consulting firm dedicated to delivering intelligent, scalable, and future-ready technology solutions. Federally incorporated in 2017 and established in Alberta in 2022, we have built a strong reputation as a reliable partner for organizations across Canada.
We operate at the intersection of data, technology, and business strategy — helping enterprises navigate complex digital landscapes with confidence. Our approach is grounded in clarity, precision, and long-term value creation, enabling our clients to modernize systems, enhance performance, and drive innovation.
